If $f_1, f_2,\dots$ is a sequence of nonnegative measurable functions in a measure space $X$, then
\[ \int_X \liminf_{n\rightarrow\infty} f_n \leq
\liminf_{n\rightarrow\infty}\int_X f_n \]
