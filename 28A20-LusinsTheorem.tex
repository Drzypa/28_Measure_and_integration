Let $A$ be a Lebesgue measurable subset of $\mathbb{R}$ and $f:A \to \mathbb{C}$ (or $f:A \to \mathbb{R}$). Then $f$ is \PMlinkname{Lebesgue measurable}{MeasurableFunctions} if and only if $f$ is almost continuous.
