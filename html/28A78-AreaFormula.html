area formula


Let ℋm denote the Hausdorff measure. Let m≤n and consider a Lipschitz function f:ℝm→ℝn. If A⊂ℝm is a Lebesgue measurable set, the equality

∫AJf⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=∫ℝnℋ0⁢(f-1⁢({y})∩A)⁢𝑑ℋm⁢y

holds, where

Jf⁢(x)=det⁡(D⁢f⁢(x)⋅D⁢f⁢(x)*)

is the Jacobian determinant of f in the point x and represent the m-volume of the image of the unit cube under the linear map D⁢f⁢(x).

If u∈L1⁢(ℝm) then one has

∫ℝmu⁢(x)⁢Jf⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=∫ℝn∑x∈f-1⁢({y})u⁢(x)⁢d⁢ℋm⁢y.

Notice that this formula is a generalization of the change of variables in integrals on ℝn.
