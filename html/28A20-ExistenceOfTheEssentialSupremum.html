existence of the essential supremum


We state the existence of the essential supremum for a set 𝒮 of extended real valued functions on a σ-finite (http://planetmath.org/SigmaFinite) measure space (Ω,ℱ,μ).

Theorem.

Suppose that the measure space (Ω,F,μ) is σ-finite. Then, the essential supremum of S exists. Furthermore, if S is nonempty then there exists a sequence (fn)n=1,2,… in S such that

esssup⁡𝒮=supn⁡fn. (1)

Note that, by reversing the inequalities, this result also applies to the essential infimum, except that equation (1) is replaced by

essinf⁡𝒮=infn⁡fn.
