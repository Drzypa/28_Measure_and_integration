computation of moment of spherical shell


In using the formula for area integration over a sphere derived in the http://planetmath.org/node/6668last example, we need to keep in mind that to every point in x⁢y plane, there correspond two points on the sphere, which are obtained by taking the two signs of the square root. The importance of this fact in obtaining a correct answer is illustrated by our next example, the calculation of the moment of inertia of a spherical shell.

The moment of a spherical shell is given by the integral

I=∫Sx2⁢d2⁢A.
