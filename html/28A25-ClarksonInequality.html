Clarkson inequality


The Clarkson inequality says that for all f,g∈Lp, for 2≤p<∞ we have:

∥f+g2∥pp+∥f-g2∥pp≤12⁢(∥f∥pp+∥g∥pp).

The inequality can be used to prove that Lp space is uniformly convex for 2≤p<∞.

Remark. If 1<p<2, then the Clarkson inequality becomes:

∥f+g2∥pq+∥f-g2∥pq≤(12⁢∥f∥pp+12⁢∥g∥pp)1p-1

.

for functions f,g∈Lp, where q=pp-1.
